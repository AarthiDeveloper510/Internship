About MITS

Micro Information Technology Services (MITS) is a leading platform helping students gain real-world experience through internships, workshops, and industry exposure. We collaborate with top universities and organizations to prepare you for a professional career.

Why Apply for Our Internships?

  • Work with real-world tools and technologies
  • Get certified and recognized by industry leaders
  • Build a portfolio that impresses recruiters

How to Apply

  1. Click on "Internship Application" in the sidebar
  2. Fill in your academic and personal details
  3. Submit the application and wait for approval
  4. Download your offer letter once selected
